KPI: PROCESS AUTOMATION

Oil and Gas, social relationship


Indicators for social management and BI

The management of social projects has a challenge: Quantify the results, measure the impacts in a numerical language that allows visualizing the return on investment and making decisions comparable to the management of different departments of a company. This project was developed together with the social management department of the operator Gran Tierra Energy CO. We organize the flow of internal information related to social incidents, design management and impact indicators and optimize the process of generating reports for making senior management decisions. Likewise, we carry out information gathering in the field and design of surveys to measure the impacts of the company's different social initiatives in the communities influenced by hydrocarbon exploration and extraction operations.

Oil and Gas, social relationship


This project under development with the support of Gran Tierra Energy CO, consists of the application of data science to detect new patterns and trends in social incidents related to the rejection of hydrocarbon exploration and extraction operations, translating these analyzes into model development. of artificial intelligence ML to guide the operational efforts of social management seeking to reduce the impacts to the company.

VIRTUAL EDUCATION, RELATIONSHIP WITH COMMUNITIES

PHI 3D - digital technology for energy pedagogy


Environmental and energy pedagogy within communities is a challenge for many companies in the sector. With this project we design new tools to transfer technical knowledge in a simplified way to indigenous communities and rural communities involved in processes of environmental License for operations of the oil industry in Colombia. We had the opportunity to do information gathering in the field with indigenous communities together with the team of professionals of the operating company, as a result a new methodology is under construction supported with 3D digital technology.

Simulation-optimized pedagogical and social relations materials

Creating a pedagogical game goes through different stages and filters, from generating a strategy that allows to transmit knowledge to fun, entertainment and motivation methods for users and players.
